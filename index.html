WHAT WE OFFER

CONSULTATION AND PLANNING

  • Personalized Consultation: Schedule a one-on-one meeting to discuss ideas and requirements.
  • Site Analysis: Evaluate the existing landscape, soil conditions, and environmental factors.
  • Design Proposal: Present a conceptual design with sketches or digital models to visualize the project.

DESIGN AND IMPLEMENTATION

  • Detailed Planning: Develop a comprehensive blueprint including plant selection, hardscape elements, and layout specifics.
  • Project Management: Coordinate the entire process, from procuring materials to managing labor and timelines.
  • Quality Execution: Ensure precision and quality in planting, installing hardscapes, and integrating water features and lighting.

MAINTANANCE AND SUPPORT

  • Regular Maintenance: Offer services like mowing, pruning, fertilizing, and pest control.
  • Seasonal Care: Prepare the landscape for different seasons with activities such as mulching, aeration, and snow removal.
  • Support and Updates: Provide tips, updates, and additional services as the landscape matures and evolves.
